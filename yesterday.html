Yesterday

  • Writer: Lennon-McCartney
  • Producer: George Martin
  • Released: August 6 1965
  • Recorded: June 17 1965

Bio

Yesterday is a song created by the English rock group The Beatles. The song was recorded on June 17 1965 and was released August 6 1965. The song is credited to Lennon-McCartney but was primarily written by Paul McCartney after he imagined the tune in a dream. The song stands today as one of The Beatles' most iconic songs and was a massive departue from their usual sound.

Here Comes The Sun

  • Writer: George Harrison
  • Producer: George Martin
  • Released: September 26 1969
  • Recorded: 7 July-19 August 1969

Bio

Here Comes The Sun is a song created by the English rock group The Beatles. The song was recorded in 1969 and was released September 26 1969. The song was written by George Harrison in Eric Clapton's garden while skipping a business meeting. The song stands today as one of The Beatles' most iconic songs and is their most listened to song on streaming platforms.

Personnel

  • George Harrison: Vocals, Acoustic guitar, Electric guitar, Moog Synthesizer, Harmonium
  • Paul McCartney: Backing vocals, Bass
  • Ringo Starr: Drums
